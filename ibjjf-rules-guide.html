What Are Advantages?
‚ûï
Advantages are awarded for nearly completing scoring positions or threatening submissions. They break ties when points are equal.

Awarded For:

  • Nearly completing a takedown
  • Positions held less than 3 seconds
  • Close submission attempts
  • Almost passing guard
  • Coming on top after both pull guard
Incomplete Positions
‚è±Ô∏è
These situations earn advantages instead of full points due to incomplete control.

Examples:

  • Mount with both arms trapped = Advantage
  • Back control with body triangle = Advantage
  • Knee on belly with other knee down = Advantage
  • Guard pass but opponent stands = Advantage
Submission Attempts
üîí
Close submission attempts that put opponent in danger can earn advantages at referee's discretion.

Referee Considers:

  • Proximity to finishing
  • Amount of danger
  • Quality of technique
  • Opponent's defensive reaction
Advantages for submissions are entirely at referee's discretion.
Tie-Breaking Order
‚öñÔ∏è
When scores are tied, this order determines the winner.

Priority:

  • 1. Points (highest priority)
  • 2. Advantages (second)
  • 3. Penalties (fewer is better)
  • 4. Referee Decision (final)
There are NEVER draws in IBJJF - a winner is always declared.
Penalty System
‚ö†Ô∏è
IBJJF uses penalties for rule violations, stalling, and dangerous actions.

Penalty Progression:

  • 1st & 2nd: Warning
  • After 2nd: Opponent gets 1 Advantage
  • 3rd Penalty: Opponent gets 2 Points
  • 4th Penalty: Disqualification
Stalling
üò¥
Not engaging with opponent or avoiding action - the most common penalty.

Examples:

  • Holding position 20+ seconds without progress
  • Running from sweeps or submissions
  • Not attempting to pass, sweep, or submit
  • Pulling guard then standing immediately
  • Leaving area to avoid techniques
Referee says "LUTE" (fight) and gives 20 seconds. No progress = penalty.
Technical Fouls
üìã
Minor rule violations that accumulate toward disqualification.

Common Fouls:

  • Gripping inside gi sleeves/pants

  • Talking during match
  • Not following referee instructions
  • Repeatedly leaving the mat
Severe Penalties
üö´
Immediate disqualification - no warnings given for these violations.

Immediate DQ:

  • Slamming opponent
  • Striking in any way
  • Biting or hair pulling
  • Illegal submissions for belt level

  • Unsportsmanlike conduct
Fleeing The Mat
üèÉ
Deliberately leaving competition area to avoid techniques.

Penalty:

  • 2 Points to Opponent
  • Match restarted standing

One of the few ways to lose 2 points immediately!
